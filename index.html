Core Objectives

Every core module from the PRD is represented in a single connected journey.

The experience keeps the emotional center on giving, while still making subscriptions, score entry, and draw participation feel rewarding and clear.

Subscription Engine

Monthly and yearly plans, charity percentage controls, renewal states, and subscriber-only access cues.

Score Experience

Five-score rolling history, reverse chronology, duplicate-date protection, and edit or delete management.

Reward Draws

Random and weighted simulation modes, jackpot rollover logic, and automated prize-tier distribution.

Charity Impact

Selectable charities, spotlight campaigns, independent donations, and transparent contribution totals.

User Journey

The product flow stays simple for subscribers and powerful for administrators.

Public visitors learn the concept, subscribers manage scores and participation, and admins control the engine behind every result.

01

Subscribe and choose a cause

Players pick a monthly or yearly plan, set a charity percentage above the 10% minimum, and lock in a beneficiary from the directory.

02

Log the last five scores

Each Stableford entry requires a date and score between 1 and 45. The platform retains only the latest five results automatically.

03

Enter the monthly reward draw

Each active subscriber participates in a monthly draw, with prize tiers based on three, four, or five matching numbers.

04

Verify winnings when required

Winners upload score proof, admins review submissions, and payout states move from pending to paid once verified.

Delivery Notes

This prototype covers the frontend behavior and product thinking the PRD calls for.

The remaining production work for a true submission is mostly backend, deployment, payment integration, and authentication hardening.

Implemented here

  • Modern charity-led landing page and product messaging
  • Interactive subscriber dashboard with score validation and 5-score rolling logic
  • Editable and removable dated scores with duplicate-date protection
  • Prize pool, charity contribution, and dashboard summaries
  • Admin draw simulator with random and weighted modes
  • Winner verification queue and payout state presentation

Needed for full production submission

  • Supabase schema, authentication, and row-level permissions
  • Stripe subscription lifecycle and webhook handling
  • Server-side draw execution, audit logs, and monthly scheduling
  • Proof uploads with secure storage and admin review actions
  • Email notifications, environment configuration, and deployment to a new Vercel account
  • Admin credentialing, real reports, and end-to-end testing
